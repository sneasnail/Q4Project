EPIC places in the Philippines~!

🇵🇭Rizal Park, Manila🇵🇭
🌾Banaue Rice Terraces, Ifugao🌾
🛶Puerto Princesa,Palawan🛶
🌊Kayangan Lake, Coron, Palawan🌊

🇵🇭 Formerly known as Bagumbayan during the Spanish Occupation, Rizal Park is an iconic location that carries with it a deep legacy,being the location where the country's National Hero, Dr. Jose Rizal was executed.

🌾 Sometimes called as the "Eighth Wonder of The World", The Banaue Rice Terraces are terraces that were carved into the mountains of Banaue, Ifugao, in the Philippines, by the ancestors of the Igorot people

🛶 The Puerto Princesa Underground River is declared as one of the New 7 Wonders of Nature. The PPSRNP contains a full mountain to the sea ecosystem and protect forests, which are among the most significant in Asia.

🌊 Kayangan Lake is a crystal-clear freshwater lake in Coron, featuring underwater rock formations, caves and islets ; Known as the cleanest lake in the entire country.
